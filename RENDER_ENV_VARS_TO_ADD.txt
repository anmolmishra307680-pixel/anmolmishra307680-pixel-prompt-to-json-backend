RENDER ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES TO ADD
=====================================

Copy and paste these into your Render dashboard:

LOCAL_GPU_ENABLED=true
COMPUTE_STRATEGY=hybrid
YOTTA_API_KEY=bhiv-yotta-production-key-2024
YOTTA_ENDPOINT=https://api.yotta.com/v1/inference
BURST_THRESHOLD=0.6
BHIV_BUCKET_ENABLED=true
BHIV_BUCKET_NAME=bhiv-production-storage
BHIV_ACCESS_KEY=BHIV_PROD_ACCESS_KEY_2024
BHIV_SECRET_KEY=bhiv-secret-storage-key-2024-production
BHIV_ENDPOINT=https://storage.bhiv.ai
BHIV_REGION=us-east-1
PYTORCH_AVAILABLE=true
RENDER=true

STEPS TO ADD:
1. Go to Render dashboard
2. Select your service
3. Go to Environment tab
4. Add each variable above
5. Click "Save Changes"
6. Redeploy

After adding these, your deployment logs should show:
✅ Local GPU: True
✅ Yotta: True  
✅ BHIV Bucket: Configured
✅ Hybrid compute strategy: Active
